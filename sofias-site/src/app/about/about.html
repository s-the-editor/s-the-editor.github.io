About

Sofia Alice is a writer, editor, and software engineer. She grew up in Melrose, Massachusetts. In 2024, she graduated with High Honors from Emory University with a BS in Computer Science and a BA in English and Creative Writing. She now lives in New Hampshire and works as a Software Engineer. Her dream is to be a full-time author of fantasy novels.

This website contains current creative projects, social media links, book recommendations, and a shop where you can purchase my art and writing.
